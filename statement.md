Problem Statement
Without an easy and interactive tool, it is cumbersome to manage personal monthly finances. Manually, users hardly monitor their income, savings, and expenses; that results in inefficient financial budgeting and planning.

Project Scope
The project presents an intuitive desktop-based application wherein users can enter their monthly income, define the percentage of savings, add several named expenses, and display a simplified budget summary. It also includes input validation with user-friendly feedback to assist in accurate financial management.

Target Users
People who want an easy tool to manage and plan monthly budgets.
Students, young professionals, and anyone new to personal finance.
Users who are comfortable with desktop applications and looking for a free, basic budgeting solution.

High-Level Features
Input fields for monthly income and savings percentage.
The ability to add several expenses with names and amounts dynamically.
Calculation of total savings, remaining balance after savings, total expenses, final balance. Display of a detailed budget summary, including the breakdown of expenses. User input validation with error and information pop-up messages. Clean interface, visually accessible, with clear labeling of buttons for ease of use.
